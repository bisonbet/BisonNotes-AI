BisonNotes AI Accessibility

BisonNotes AI is designed to support common recording, transcription, summary, and settings tasks with Apple accessibility features on iPhone, iPad, Mac Catalyst, and Apple Watch.

Supported Features

  • VoiceOver labels, values, and hints for recording controls, imports, playback, transcripts, summaries, settings, and Apple Watch recording.
  • Voice Control-friendly action names for common in-app tasks, including contextual recording, transcript, and summary actions.
  • Dynamic Type through SwiftUI system text styles and wrapping layouts.
  • System colors and text status cues so important state is not conveyed by color alone.
  • Reduce Motion support for recording indicators on iPhone, iPad, Mac Catalyst, and Apple Watch.
  • Keyboard-accessible settings and task flows on iPad with hardware keyboard and Mac Catalyst.

Known Limitations

  • BisonNotes creates transcripts, but transcripts are not time-synchronized captions for all audio playback. Captions are not claimed for this version.
  • Audio descriptions are not supported in this version.
  • Hardware flows such as Apple Watch transfer, real microphone capture, iCloud sync, Control Center, and Action Button require device validation.
  • External provider account pages or system share sheets may have accessibility behavior controlled by Apple or the provider.

Contact

For accessibility feedback, use the support path on the BisonNotes AI page. Include your device model, OS version, BisonNotes app version, the assistive technology used, and the task that did not work as expected.
